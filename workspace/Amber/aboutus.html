Our philosophy:

We are a small company that embraces our responsibility to co-create a world where each of us, our communities and our planet can flourish, be healthy and look good while doing it.

By buying locally organic food and humanely made clothing we hope for a sustainable future. This means our children and grandchildren will be living in a world that values human creativity, diversity, and individual choice.

Our focus is on food and products for children because healthy habits need to start at a very young age.

About our food source

We looked for a reliable source of food raised or grown without the use of persistent pesticides. We are aware ofthe effects of pesticides and fertilizers on our soil and waterways.

Do you agree that local, sustainably grown food can actually strengthen our communities? Then organic farming which is a method of agriculture that pays proper respect to our health, the environment and the important role of food within society

About our clothing

We strive to find apparel and accessories are made of only all natural fibers, including organic cotton, organic alpaca, wool, silk, and other fibers created by nature. Conventional cotton is one of the most pesticide-dependent crops. Organic cotton and natural coloring can be used to produce beautiful fashion that is free of all pesticides.

Our commitment to find products of all natural fibers and low-impact dyes stems from the desire to ensure clothing manufacturing process is environmentally friendly, and never harms water tables and local communities.
